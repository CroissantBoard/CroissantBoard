Follow 3 simple steps to begin

Register Account

First you need to register new account. Sign Up if you still don't have an account with us or simply log in. You can use either basic form registration or access via your google or facebook account! Pretty simple:)

Create meeting

In your personal account find a control bar to your left. Here you can create new meetings, schedule them and invite people to join it.

Communicate

At the due time just enter the meeting and start discussing things with your colleagues. See - it's as easy as possible! Enjoy;)
